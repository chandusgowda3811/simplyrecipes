Greek Ribs

Delicious Greek-style ribs that are quick and easy to make.

Prep : 15min | Cook : 5min

Instructions

Step 1

Marinate the ribs in a mixture of Greek yogurt, olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, and your favorite Greek spices. Let them marinate for at least 1 hour or overnight in the refrigerator.

Step 2

Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. Remove the ribs from the marinade and grill for about 5 minutes on each side, or until they are cooked to your desired level of doneness.

Step 3

Serve the Greek ribs hot with a side of tzatziki sauce and garnish with fresh herbs. Enjoy your homemade Greek ribs!

Ingredients

2 pounds pork ribs

1/2 cup Greek yogurt

2 tablespoons olive oil

Juice of 1 lemon

2 cloves garlic, minced

Greek spices (e.g., oregano, thyme, rosemary)

Salt and pepper to taste

Tools

Grill

Tongs

Mixing Bowl

Measuring Spoons
