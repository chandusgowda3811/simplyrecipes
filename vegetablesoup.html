Vegetable Soup

Delicious and healthy vegetable soup that's quick and easy to make.

Prep: 15 min | Cook: 5 min

Instructions

Step 1

In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onions, carrots, and celery. Cook until softened, about 5 minutes.

Step 2

Stir in vegetable broth, diced tomatoes, and a variety of your favorite vegetables. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes until the vegetables are tender.

Step 3

Season with salt, pepper, and any desired herbs or spices. Serve hot and enjoy your homemade vegetable soup!

Ingredients

1 tbsp olive oil

1 onion, chopped

2 carrots, diced

2 celery stalks, chopped

4 cups vegetable broth

1 can diced tomatoes

Assorted vegetables of your choice

Salt and pepper to taste

Herbs and spices (optional)

Tools

Large Pot

Knife and Cutting Board

Stirring Spoon
