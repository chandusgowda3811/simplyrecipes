Carne Asada

Enjoy the flavors of grilled Carne Asada with this simple recipe.

Prep : 15min | Cook : 5min

Instructions

Step 1

In a bowl, combine lime juice, orange juice, garlic, cilantro, cumin, salt, and pepper to create the marinade.

Step 2

Place the flank steak in a resealable plastic bag and pour the marinade over it. Seal the bag and marinate the steak in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes or up to 4 hours.

Step 3

Preheat your grill to high heat. Remove the steak from the marinade and grill for about 2-3 minutes per side, or until it reaches your desired level of doneness.

Ingredients

2 pounds flank steak

1/4 cup lime juice

1/4 cup orange juice

3 cloves garlic, minced

2 tablespoons fresh cilantro, chopped

1 teaspoon ground cumin

Salt and pepper to taste

Tools

Grill

Resealable Plastic Bag

Mixing Bowl
